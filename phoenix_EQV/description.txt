In Phoenix Equinox V, play as a legendary, powerful Phoenix of myth in a world ravaged by the scourge that is mankind. As you rise from the ashes of rebirth, explore the immersive world around you and discover the crimes against the Earth committed by mankind. After witnessing the destruction wreaked upon the once-beautiful landscape, find a way to fight back. Navigate platforming puzzles and use your mystical power to revive animals slain by the agents of man. Discover how the world is affected by human actions, and reflect on how one individual can help improve the world.
